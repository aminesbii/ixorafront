Our Philosophy

The dermocosmetic brand that understands your skin

Founded in Tunisia by Dr. Nadia Belhaj, IXORA bridges the gap between clinical-grade dermatology and the rich botanical heritage of the Mediterranean. Every formula is developed in our lab using advanced active ingredients, rigorously tested for safety and efficacy.

We believe your skin deserves more than empty promises. That's why we avoid fillers, harsh preservatives, and unnecessary fragrances. Instead, we focus on what works — concentrated actives at their optimal levels, delivered in sensorial textures you'll look forward to using every day.

From our first serum to our latest collection, every product carries the same commitment: transparency, integrity, and results you can see.

Clean Ingredients

Thoughtfully sourced, scientifically proven

We partner with sustainable farms across the Mediterranean for our botanical extracts — from Tunisian rosemary to Moroccan argan oil. Every ingredient is traceable, ethically harvested, and selected for its proven dermatological benefits.

Our packaging is 100% recyclable, and we offset our carbon footprint through reforestation partnerships in North Africa. Beauty should never come at the cost of the planet.
